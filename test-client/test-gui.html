Cloaks Gambit GUI Test

Interactive Game Board

Click on squares to select move positions. The board shows:

  • White squares: White pieces (with symbols for known pieces)
  • Black squares: Black pieces (with symbols for known pieces)
  • Grey squares: Empty positions
  • Blue outline: Last move "from" position
  • Red outline: Last move "to" position

Side State Information

This area shows:

  • Daggers: Current dagger counts for both players
  • My Stash: Pieces available for placement
  • My On Deck: Piece currently on deck
  • Captured Pieces: Pieces taken by opponent
